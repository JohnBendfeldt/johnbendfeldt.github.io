About Me


I was born in Tanzania where my parents worked until I was five years old. I lived in Virginia until 2015 when my lovely wife, Andi, and I moved here to Austin, Texas, with our four cats. We were drawn to the warm weather and wanted to find a place to settle down. We love living in Austin.


I spent five years in management with Domino's in Virginia and Texas. My experience includes managing a staff of up to thirty team members as well as maintaining five-star operations while marketing to maintain consistent sales growth. I enjoyed working with a team to help create satisfied customers, while being self-motivated to find solutions to challenges that arose.


I was looking for a way to express myself creatively when I was introduced to coding. I enrolled in the Full-Stack Coding Boot Camp at University of Texas at Austin. My dream is to be a full-stack web developer.
